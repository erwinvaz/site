Privacy First

We do not collect any personal information from users.

Your use of the app is completely private and anonymous.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

The "How Old Are You" app was developed with a total focus on user privacy. We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Personal data (name, email, phone, address)
  • Geographic location
  • Contacts or phone lists
  • Photos or device files
  • Usage or behavior data
  • Device identifiers

2. App Permissions

Internet Access

The app requests internet access exclusively to:

  • Display this Privacy Policy
  • Load advertisements (when applicable)

No personal data is transmitted through this connection.

⚠️ Important

All age calculations are performed locally on your device. The birth dates you enter are not sent to any external server.

3. Cookies and Tracking

This app does NOT use cookies.

We do not implement any tracking, analytics, or user behavior monitoring technologies.

4. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no information to share with third parties.

The app may display ads provided by Google AdMob. Ads may collect anonymous usage information according to Google's privacy policy.

5. Children's Protection

This app is not specifically intended for children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of information from minors.

6. Security

Although we do not collect personal data, we implement security measures to protect the integrity of the app and ensure a safe experience for all users.

7. App Cost

The app is available free of charge to all users.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the update date.

We recommend that you review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

9. Your Rights

You can control app permissions through your device settings. If you choose to disable internet access, some features (such as viewing this policy or ads) may not work properly.

10. Contact

Website: www.talento.dev.br
